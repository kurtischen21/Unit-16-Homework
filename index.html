NON-PROFIT

Food Finders is a non-profit organization that is based in Lakewood, CA.

Food Finders specializes in food recovery, which is locating and receiving food from people or restaurants, and distributing food to pantries or shelters that need it most.

The goal was to revamp the information architecture, improve the feel and flow of the donation process, and to deliver their cause in a more impactful manner.
